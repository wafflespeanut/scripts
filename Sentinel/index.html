NOTE:

  • To the newbies, this is an encryption cipher. If you've forgot your key (password), then there's no "straight-forward" way you can recover your message from the ciphertext (but, you're always welcome to workaround). Even a slight variation in the key will lead you to stupid characters...
    (I've written more about that!)

  • Again, this runs entirely on your browser. If you were to run my original algorithm, higher order iterations and gigantic random keys may crash your browser!

    So, I've simplified (reducing security to improve performance) the original version of Sentinel (written in Python). Random keygen, and iterations have been "confiscated" (for demo purposes)...
