NOTE:

  • To the newbies, this is an encryption cipher. If you've forgot your key (password), then there's no "straight-forward" way you can recover your message from the ciphertext (but, you're always welcome to workaround). Even a slight variation in the key will lead you to stupid characters... (I've written more about that!)

  • Again, this runs entirely on your browser. If you were to run my original algorithm, higher order iterations and gigantic random key generation may crash your browser!

    • So, I've simplified the original version written in Python (reducing security at the cost of performance). Hence, some of those functions have been "confiscated" (for demo purposes)
